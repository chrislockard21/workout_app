About the app

                

                

                    Your health is important! The goal of this applicaiton is to give you a fun and interactive
                    way to track your workouts to help you get to and keep going to the gym. Statisctics on your workout
                    provide insight on your performance and help to keep you motivated. Set tracking allows you to enter detailed
                    information on each set so that you can improve. A complete workout history allows you to see any of your past
                    workouts and will stick around even after you delete a routine.
                

                
Everyone should be able to get in shape and feel better about themselves. My goal with this workout app is to help you do that!
